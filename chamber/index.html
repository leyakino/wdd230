WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL SITE OF THE ANGONO CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

Greetings from the Art Capital of the Philippines!

Known as one of the major art centers of the Philippines, the Municipality of Angono sticks to its commitment of setting this town into an ideal place to live in - healthy and peaceful, nourished and flourished by different forms of art, endowed with cultural treasures and local government innovations. It is with great pride to present and hand in to you the Angono Chamber of Commerce that contains small, medium, and large enterprises, local chambers and industry associations representing various sectors of business, all working together to foster a healthier economy and improve the viability of business in the this small tranquil and artistic town can offer.

The Angono Chamber of Commerce and Industry is a non-government association compose of private businesses that advocates business growth and sustainable development by providing business services for the advancement of grassroots entrepreneurship, chamber development, international trade relations and business innovation.

Trade fair opens at SM City Taytay

In line with the triple celebration of the 110th Araw ng Lalawigan ng Rizal, Independence Day and Dr. Jose Rizal’s birth anniversary, the provincial government with the Rizal Small and Medium Enterprise Development (RISMED) Council, the Rizal Exporters and Manufacturers’ Association, Inc. (REMAI) and the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)-Rizal opened the Alay sa Lalawigan Trade Fair this morning, Dec 29, at SM City Taytay.
