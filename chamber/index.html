WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL SITE OF THE ANGONO CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

Greetings from the Art Capital of the Philippines!

Known as one of the major art centers of the Philippines, the Municipality of Angono sticks to its commitment of setting this town into an ideal place to live in - healthy and peaceful, nourished and flourished by different forms of art, endowed with cultural treasures and local government innovations. It is with great pride to present and hand in to you the Angono Chamber of Commerce that contains small, medium, and large enterprises, local chambers and industry associations representing various sectors of business, all working together to foster a healthier economy and improve the viability of business in the this small tranquil and artistic town can offer.
